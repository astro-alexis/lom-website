About

Lappkärrsbergets områdesförening (LOm) is the tenant association of Lappis. Our purpose is to act as a mediator between the Lappis tenants and SSSB regarding everything that relates to our living situation. Other than that we are also tasked with managing facilities around Lappis that are used for events and some can be gained access to by all SSSB tenants.

LOm is organized in sections that are themselves often bound to a certain facility that they manage. These sections are spread out all around Lappis and they offer a variety of activities and services.

In you have any questions or want to get in touch with the board we encourage you to contact us on info@lom.nu or follow us on Facebook and Instagram!

Sections

Pica

Adress: Forskarbacken 14. Instagram: @studiopica. Facebook: Studio Pica. Booking: link

Studio Pica can be rented for private events and also has a long history of organizing public events such as Open Mics and International dinners.

The place is well equipped with furniture, sound system, some lighting and a kitchen. The rent for cafe pica is 500 kr and has a deposit of 3000 kr.

The booking page is here, and there you can find all details on how to book it and use it. You can also contact us at pica@lom.nu

​Family room

Adress: Amanuensvägen 5B. Booking: link

The Family room is a small venue where you can have quiet get-togethers with your friends or family. The place is perfect for dinner parties, meetings, children’s birthday parties. The room is in a residential building and therefore loud parties are not allowed on the premises.

A maximum number of 20 people can use the place and there are tables and chairs that accommodate this amount of people. There is a small kitchen with stove plates and various kitchenware. We have an indoor shoes policy: you are welcome to use our slippers or please bring your indoor shoes.

The booking page is here, and there you can find all details on how to book it and use it. You can also contact us at family@lom.nu

​Movie room

Adress: Forskarbacken 10 (basement). Facebook: Lappis Movie Room. Booking: link

The Lappis Movie room is a room with projector and sofas. You can book it for a cozy movie night with your friends, or come by our periodic events, where we’ll show interesting movies and discuss afterwards (free popcorn included!).

The booking page is here, and there you can find all details on how to book it and use it. You can also contact our lovely team at cinema@lom.nu if you have any questions or follow us on Facebook!

Gym

Adress: Professorsslingan 13 & Forskarbacken 5. Website: lappisgym.se

We provide the residents of Lappkärrsberget with a cheap, well equipped and close by alternative. Two facilities are maintained and available to members. The main gym is situated in the stairways below the house caretaker office at Professorsslingan 13, and a cardio room offering plenty of open space and cardio machines is located in Forskarbacken 5.

For more information about the gym and on how to become a member visit us at lappisgym.se

Kostnix

Address: Professorsslingan 39 (basement). Facebook: KostNix Lappis Opening hours: mondays from 20.00 to 22.00

Kostnix is a non commercial store in the middle of Lappkärrsberget.

​We collect and give away old things for absolutely free; furniture, clothes, electrical equipment, kitchen ware, toys, shoes and so on.

​There is no money involved. Kostnix is run by volunteers and exists for the benefit of all of Lappis students.

We are always looking for volunteers. Whether you want to help fight consumerism or just come and hang out with us, you are more than welcome to stop by! Find us on Facebook!

Music room

Address: Professorsslingan 15. Facebook: Lappis.Music.Room

The Lappis Music Room is an underground place for you and your band to enjoy as a tenant of Lappis! Here we have a drum kit, a handful of amps and guitars, a keyboard, some microphones, a PA system and vibes. Basically, everything you need to hone your craft and practice for your next performance!

Membership is 150 kr per semester, on top of a 400 kr key deposit, which is returned after you revoke your membership. Opening hours are between 10:00 and 21:00.

Booking is handled through a booking system once you have received your key.

For more info about membership and keys, check out our Facebook group and email to music@lom.nu.

Workshop

Address: Forskarbacken 6 . Facebook: LOm Workshop

The workshop is a place for those who love practical hobbies and crafts. Here you can swing by to fix your bike, work of a carpentry project, or perhaps you’re into 3d-printing? We offer a wide range of possibilities, its only your creativity and laziness that sets the boundaries!

We hope that you will find your creative side and join us!

You can reach us at workshop@lom.nu or follow us on Facebook!

Do not complain about the Swedish winter, live it!

Sauna

Adress: Forskarbacken 5 (north-west entrance). Booking: link

Mysigt! Lappis offers to all the residents the use of its own local sauna with a traditional wooden sauna. The sauna can accommodate eight people but it is more comfortable if you are only 4 or 5. A dressing room and hot showers are available. The thermal elements are easy to operate and all necessary rules and instructions are posted in the local. We will also show you how it works when you come to get the access key.

The booking page is here, and there you can find all details on how to book it and use it. You can also contact us at sauna@lom.nu

Address: Forskarbacken 5. The entrance door is on the North West side of the building facing the wood.

Dance room

Adress: Professorsslingan 10. Facebook: Lappis Dance Room by LOm Booking: link

One of LOm biggest facility is the dance room, located at Professorsslingan 10. Tenants can participate to dance classes that are held on a weekly basis for free.

The dance room is also available for private bookings. You can find our booking system here.

You can contact us at dance@lom.nu for further info. You are more than welcome to join us. You can also follow our instagram page for latest updates.
